What to expect when working with me...

Costs

My fees are set on a per–project basis. I have to get an idea of the details of the project before I can give you an accurate estimate. Just so you have a ballpark, the minimum for a new website is $4,500.

I also have a "subscription" option: for a flat, monthly fee you basically plug me into your internal team and I give you everything I can offer to help you meet your goals. This subscription starts at $2,500/month.

Process

Over time, I’ve realized that there isn’t a single process that's perfect for every project. So, I try to be flexible and adapt to whatever each project calls for because I find that leads to a better output. I usually do thorough research at the beginning, then quickly create wireframes and moodboards before jumping right into actual prototypes.

Timelines are normally between two and six months, but can vary greatly depending on you and your project's requirements.

Relationship

Our relationship is very important to me so I'll spend some time getting to know you. Some things to know about me:

I like a challenge. I work for your customers so sometimes I side with them. I'm a partner, not a supplier, so trust me; that's why you hired me. I like to explore questions before giving answers. I try to be honest and direct – you should be as well. I go all–in on every project.
